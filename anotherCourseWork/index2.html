About Our Project

Team Innovators is developing a lightweight monitoring system called Smart Freezer Alerts, designed for small kiosk and shop owners who need to keep track of freezer temperatures. Even short temperature rises can lead to food spoilage and financial loss, so our system provides quick alerts, a simple status dashboard, and basic sensor health monitoring.

The goal is to help owners react before problems become costly. By focusing on reliability and ease of use, Smart Freezer Alerts gives small businesses an accessible way to monitor their equipment without needing complicated tools or technical knowledge.
